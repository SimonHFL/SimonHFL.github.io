I’m a second year industrial PhD student collaborating with Siteimprove under the supervision of Anders Søgaard at the CoAStaL group, University of Copenhagen, Denmark.
The focus of my PhD currently is on improving the quality of Grammatical Error Correction systems, specifically focusing on low-resource approaches. I am also interested in the use of reinforcement learning as a way to improve NLP systems.
